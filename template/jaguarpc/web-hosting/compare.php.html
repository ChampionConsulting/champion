Semi-Dedicated Hosting:

JaguarPC invented the term "semi-dedicated" many years ago to describe a revolutionary platform which others have since tried to copy. Using our exclusive semi-dedicated hosting package we have combined the fully managed and simple to use shared environment with significant resource increases over typical shared service. Our semi-dedicated servers provide you power of a dedicated server at a fraction of the cost and without having to manage a server, maintain security, or any other hassle that comes with dedicated and private servers. Our semi-dedicated platforms have far less users than a standard shared web server and have a failover ability to provide 100% uptime even during a hardware failure. That translates to at least 6x more resources for your website and 100% uptime!
