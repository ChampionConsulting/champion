Managed VPS Hosting

Every VPS that includes a control panel addon also comes complete with our fully managed vps service. Our highly trained and experienced staff have been providing managed vps services since the introduction of vps. JaguarPC was a pioneer in the vps hosting industry giving us access to certain partner channels and other experiences that have defined vps hosting today.


It all starts with our hardware. As we provision our new vps hosting nodes we deploy powerful Supermicro systems with Intel xeon multi-cpu multi-core processors, registered ECC ram, and 8x raid-10 drive arrays. This helps ensures your services will never be waiting or competing for cpu time but also makes our job easier since we don't often have to track heavy resource users. Throw everything you can at our systems and they keep on trucking.


Next we prepare them using proprietary methods developed over years of experience. We have been providing hosting services since 1998 and have a large staff of skilled admins. This helps keep our workload down and thus your support from us faster.



We take pride in the fact that we go the extra mile to deliver top quality performance everytime. Last but not least we load your VPS with powerful vps management software from a world recognized leader in virtualization software. We offer operating systems such as CentOS, Debian, Suse, and Fedora. You can also add on a range of control panels such as Interworx, Cpanel, DirectAdmin and more!

Note Details

System Adminstration - Your vps includes 2 (two) full hours of free system administration service each month. This may be applied to any changes, updates, or any system adminstrator task you want done. Service record in 15 min intervals and regular support rates of $75/hr apply after you have used your free time.

O/S installs - We will install the initial OS, Control panel, and related server wide services. OS re-installs will cost a one time fee of $15. It takes time and work to reinstall our custom setups.

Server Updates - We will only update software and applications that affect the entire vps and OS. For example, if you would like a new module added to Apache for your vps. What we will not cover are script installs that are just for one site such as installing a blog or forum.

Security - We provide you an initially hardened and secure environment by installing various tools by request. Some things may not be compatible with your OS choice and custom setup. Please ask support about your particular wishes.
