VPS Hosting - The power of a dedicated server at a fraction of the cost

If shared hosting is ideal for a start-up business and a dedicated server is ideal for websites with large quantities of traffic, VPS hosting is designed for businesses and individuals who need more than shared hosting, but don't want to the price tag of dedicated hosting. We can quickly set up your Windows VPS web hosting or Linux VPS web hosting service on a shared hardware server, but with allocated system resources that you can depend on as your business grows.

Every VPS plan from JaguarPC features generous allocations of disk space, bandwidth and RAM, with burst RAM to protect your hosting account against unpredictable traffic spikes. In addition, at JaguarPC we are committed to never overselling our hardware, helping you to maintain consistent VPS hosting performance, regardless of what your neighbors are doing.
