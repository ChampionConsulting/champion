Champion Consulting vps servers offer multiple locations throughout the globe. We have datacenters located in Texas, Georgia, California, and the UK. We strive to provide the most reliable network by utilizing the best equipment consisting of Cisco with gigabit connections to providers such as Savvis, Abovenet, Nlayer, Mzima, Comcast, WvFiber, Onering + AIX peers.

Our vps networks utilize Avaya Route Science route optimizer to ensure the highest performance routing to your VPS versus normal BGP. While normal BGP simply looks at how many hops from internet user to your VPS, the route science system traces back to the internet user up to 7,000 times a minute identifying any problematic areas such as speed, packetloss, amount of hops than re-routing the connection to ensure the fastest possible route to your VPS.

SuperMicro Servers

Champion Consulting enterprise class servers are rack mounted Supermicro servers. Due to the nature of adding new servers and rate at which specs change we have many servers with many specs (Quad core CPu's, Hex core CPU's). But one what remains consistent is every VPS Node is running Raid 10 drive arrays, fully buffered ECC RAM, the latest Intel processors, and Hot-swap drives. Servers are designed to minimize downtime for your vps server during drive or other hardware failures.

Virtualization Software

Champion Consulting vps nodes are powered by industry leading OpenVZ coupled with SolusVM management software to power your VPS. OpenNV creates virtual environments on a single server (VPS Node) ensuring that your VPS is isolated with your own system resources making it suitable for high I/O applications.
