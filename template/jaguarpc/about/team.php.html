Greg Landis - CEO:

President & CEO of the company, is the founder of the organization back in 1998. Greg has led its development and advancement from a one man startup, to one of the industry's leading web service providers. In the summer of 2002 Greg took the company to Houston,TX where it blossomed from a small time business to one of the worlds leading shared web hosting providers. In 2010 Greg established the first major headquarters for JaguarPC in Colorado Springs, CO. While JaguarPC is Greg's passion, his inspiration is his family and kids. In his free time (yea right) he enjoys outdoor activities, including fishing, racing, and is an avid music lover.
