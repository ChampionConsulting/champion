We're Hiring: Start Your Web Hosting Career Today!

If you enjoy a fast-paced environment with ample opportunity for growth, JaguarPC is looking for you! We offer competitive salaries with a comprehensive benefits package. This is your chance to join a rapidly growing and well established company with sound morals and integrity. If your only looking for a paycheck this probably isn't the place for you. However if you want a challenging and very rewarding career then JaguarPC is the answer.


While we are headquartered in Colorado Springs, CO we do still offer both onsite and remote positions for all openings. We provide a relaxed environment, comfortable dress code, and comprehensive screening and training programs. It is our staff that has made us the success story we are today. We take great pride in our reputation, staff and the unique skill set of each individual we bring into the family.

Available Positions

Customer Service Representative

Customer Service representatives are an integral part of our success at JaguarPC. We are always looking for dependable team players who have excellent customer care skills. Self motivated people pleasers who have the drive to go above and beyond the call of duty are in demand. Are you the rare prized worker who takes pride in your work? If you have strong core values, a solid work ethic and are meticulous about spelling and grammar skills then we may be a good fit for you.


Technical Support Representative

JaguarPC is a top ranked web hosting company that is growing quickly. We are currently seeking friendly, knowledgeable and dedicated technical support representatives who are determined to provide excellent support to both our customers and fellow team members. If you want to learn new skills and improve your current ones then we may be a good fit for you. Start your new career today by entering into the fast paced world of web hosting. We can help put you on a high speed track to success. All shifts available.


System Administrator

Are ready to provide amazing customer and server experiences? JaguarPC may be the perfect environment to showcase your server installation and maintenance skills. If you like making servers sing, pouncing on issues before they become bigger issues, making thousands of customers happy and learning new ways to do things then this is the position for you. All shifts available.
