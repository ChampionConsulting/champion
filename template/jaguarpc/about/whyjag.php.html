30 Day Moneyback Guarantee:

The JaguarPC.com brand stands behind everything we do. We are so confident in our ability to satisfy your web hosting needs that we back all of our shared hosting plans with an unconditional 30 day money back guarantee. If for any reason during the first 30 days you are not completely satisfied for any reason, you may cancel your account and we will refund any hosting package fee that we have collected.

Instant Setup:

Our system creates shared hosting accounts automatically. Your new web hosting account will be created within minutes of placing an order and making a payment. You will then receive your account activation email along with account login details so that you can start uploading and managing your new account. Customers who have ordered VPS or Dedicated servers will still have their accounts setups almost instantly however it requires some manual intervention.
