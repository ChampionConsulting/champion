Joomla Hosting

Joomla Hosting, commonly known as Joomla, is an excellent Content Management System (CMS) for building websites and other powerful online applications. Joomla is an open source solution that is freely available to everybody and is used all over the world for enriching simple and personal homepages, along with several complex corporate web applications.

Installation and Features of Joomla Hosting

Joomla Hosting is one of the simplest programs to install. Its user-friendly features make it easy to manage. The system is highly reliable and can be effectively used across various applications. Some of the useful applications of Joomla include:

» Preparing corporate websites or portals.
» Online commerce
» Small business websites, non-profit and organizational websites
» Government applications
» Corporate intranets and extranets
» School and church websites
» Personal or family homepages
» Community-based portals
» Magazine and newspaper websites

Joomla Hosting can be conveniently used for adding content and images to update a product catalog or for taking online reservations. A Joomla Host offers a continuous growth path for your web site or web hosting business.
